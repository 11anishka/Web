Cascade Home Page

The default home page upon logging in features customizable widgets. The default widgets is one click away from recently accessed blocks, pages, etc. Follow the instructions on picture to switch websites.

Switching Sites

After clicking on the area indicated on the navigation bar the following dropdown will appear allowing access to the following sites: AGF, AGF Files, AGF CMS.
